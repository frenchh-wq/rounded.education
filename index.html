The expectation just changed. The capacity didn't.

The DfE Enrichment Framework (published 15 June 2026) sets 8 benchmarks across 5 categories every school is expected to deliver. Ofsted considers it from September 2026 under Personal Development, and new parent-facing school profiles put it in the shop window.

But there's no new funding guaranteed, staffing is stretched, and leaders are already cutting trips and clubs. You're being asked to plan, deliver, evidence and improve an enrichment offer — with no extra time. That's the gap Rounded closes.
