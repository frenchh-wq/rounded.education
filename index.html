Four levels. Language Ofsted already uses.

Each of the 8 benchmarks is scored on a four-point scale — so your report maps directly to inspection vocabulary.

Developing
Intent exists, delivery is limited. You have plans but provision is inconsistent or undocumented.
1 pt
Establishing
Consistent delivery, patchy evidence. Provision is happening but impact isn't yet captured.
2 pts
Securing
Strong delivery and clear evidence. Minimum threshold for The Enrichment Quality Mark.
3 pts
Exemplary
Sector-leading, replicable practice. Your approach is worth sharing with other schools.
4 pts
